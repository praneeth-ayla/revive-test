About Us

Revive is a provider of world class integrated business, technology and process solutions on a global delivery platform. Revive Software Systems is a global business consulting company that provides services in Business functions and Information Technology. We believe that today’s Technology is constructing tomorrows’ life. Leveraging functional and domain expertise, global delivery model and our technology practices, we enable companies to unlock the business potential.

We are powered by a pool of innovative and talented professionals excelling in enterprise solutions, client relationship management, business intelligence, business process quality, operations management, engineering solutions, product lifecycle management and infrastructure services among other key capabilities. Our functions are majorly in Supply Chain Management (SCM), Financial Management, Human Capital Management (HCM), Customer Relationship Management(CRM), Supply Relationship Management (SRM), Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) and Corporate Performance Management (CPM).We also support customers with expertise in Web Services, SAP Net Weaver Technologies, Process Integration, Portals, Business Intelligence, Business Objects and Cognos.
Our comprehensive consulting, system integration and outsourcing services provide customers with affordable and flexible approach to solving business problems, thereby accelerating success and improving performance. Revive Software Systems provides customized, upgraded Application Maintenance (AMS) and support services that meet the needs of the customers across the globe.
